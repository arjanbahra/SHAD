Here are some tips!

Find a Real Problem

Start by identifying a problem or opportunity you're passionate about solving. Consider issues in your community or broader society where innovative solutions are needed.

Create a Clear Plan

Outline specific goals, steps, and timelines for your initiative. Define how your idea will address the identified problem. Consider the resources and support you'll need, such as mentors or materials.

Use Digital Platforms

Showcase your initiative on the organization's website or social media. Share progress and engage with their community for feedback. Increased online visibility can attract funding from corporations interested in supporting youth-led projects.
